Philosopher's Cave

Thinking about ways of thinking. This collection explores the existentialism, perception and existence as themes. Inspired by philosophers like Plato, Socrates, Albert Camus, Nietzsche and so on. The selection of visuals, texts, and digital artifacts represent the dilemmas of illusion and truth, self and system, control and freedom, world as we assume it.
